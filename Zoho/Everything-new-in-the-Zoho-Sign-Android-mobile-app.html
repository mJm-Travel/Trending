Everything new in the Zoho Sign Android mobile app

Posted on April 14, 2025 by Nandhitha M
3 Mins Read

With a 4.5 star user rating in the Google Play Store, Zoho Sign's native mobile app for Android is a must-have for secure, swift, and hassle-free paperwork management. The app's user-friendly interface makes it the signature choice for rapidly growing digital businesses, and we're excited to announce improved features and functionalities.

Discover our latest additions to the Zoho Sign experience from the comfort of your Android mobile screens!

  1. Improved user interface

The app features a new look, with added menus and a more intuitive interface. What's more, you can switch between dark and light mode, enable automatic field guidance to avoid scrolling, and hide the screen when accessing other apps to ensure top-tier security when signing documents.

         

                               Old user interface                                               New user interface                              

  1. Sign securely with QES via InfoCert and Uanataca

Qualified electronic signatures (QES) via qualified trust service providers such as InfoCert and Uanataca are now at your fingertips. Sign via QES with Zoho Sign to ensure legal compliance for your digitally signed documents in the EU and maintain industry-standard compliance.

  1. Sign in 22 languages with four new additions

With four new languages topping up our existing multilingual support, Android users can now access the interface, emails, and even sign in Thai, Vietnamese, Bahasa Indonesian, and Traditional Chinese. Learn about vernacular signing

  1. Activate visible signatures

Enable visible signatures to catalogue metadata on every signed document. Recording signer metadata—such as their name, a unique recipient ID, email address, reason for signing, and date of signing—helps you comply with industry standards and ensure an additional layer of security by enforcing traceability.

  1. Scan and upload files from the native framework scanner

You no longer have to rely on third-party scanner apps to upload documents for signing. Zoho Sign's Android app comes with a native framework scanner that helps you scan and upload documents directly from your device.

  1. Email completed documents after in-person signing

You can now email in-person signers the completed documents straight through the Android app.

  1. Dynamic knowledge-based authentication (KBA) for US data center

Android users in the US data center can verify their recipient's identity using dynamic KBA, provided the the integration with IDology. Recipients will be prompted to answer questions that only they know the answers to, with information drawn from the social security database. This guarantees foolproof signer authentication.

  1. Collect payments with the payment signer field

Collect payments and signatures on the go in a single workflow. Once you integrate Zoho Checkout with Zoho Sign, simply add a payment field to the document to initiate payment collection.

  1. Log in from multiple accounts

Zoho Sign imposes no restrictions on the number of users who can access the same Android app, so feel free to log in with multiple Zoho Sign accounts and switch between them with a single click.

Excited to try these updates? Keep an eye out for our monthly release notes and subscribe to our social media channels to be the first to know of any app updates.
