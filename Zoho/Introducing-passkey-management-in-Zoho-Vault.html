Introducing passkey management in Zoho Vault

Posted on August 7, 2025 by Lindsey
4 Mins Read

For more than a decade, Zoho Vault has been a trusted solution for businesses to securely store, share, and manage their passwords. Back in 2018, we envisioned a passwordless future by introducing passwordless single sign-on (SSO) for cloud applications. Today, we’re excited to take another big step forward—Zoho Vault now supports passkey management!

At Zoho, we’re committed to innovation and security, ensuring that businesses have access to the most convenient and secure authentication methods. We’ve already implemented passkey authentication for Zoho Accounts, and now, with Zoho Vault, you can store and manage all of your passkeys in one secure location.

In this blog post, we’ll explore what passkeys are, why they're the future of authentication, and how Zoho Vault helps you transition to a truly passwordless experience.

What are passkeys?

Passkeys are the new method of authentication to protect our accounts. Instead of the traditional password, a passkey allows you to log into your accounts (including Zoho Vault) without having to memorize or type in anything. With support from major tech companies like Apple, Zoho, Google, and Microsoft, passkeys are becoming the new standard for passwordless authentication.

Why should you switch to passkeys ?

Built-in security

Passkeys are automatically generated with a public–private key pair. You don’t have to worry about creating a strong password because each passkey is designed to be highly secure from the start.

No more passwords to remember

With passkeys, all you need is biometric authentication (like your fingerprint or face ID) or your device passcode to sign in—nothing to type, nothing to remember. Logging in is faster and simpler than ever.

Your private key remains private

Your private key is never shared with the websites you log into. While the public key is stored on the website, it’s useless on its own, so your account stays safe—even if the website’s servers are compromised.

Protection against data breaches

If a website gets hacked, the only thing the attackers can access is the public key—no private key, no passwords to steal. This means your accounts are safe, even in the event of a data breach.

Built-in protection against phishing and social engineering attacks

Passkeys provide strong protection against phishing and social engineering attacks. Unlike passwords, which can be easily stolen via fake websites, passkeys use WebAuthn to ensure that your credentials are shared only with trusted sites, keeping scammers at bay.

A better user experience

Forget about the frustration of typing out passwords. Passkeys are faster, easier, and more convenient, letting you spend less time logging in and more time enjoying the services you love.  

Which websites and apps support passkeys?  

Passkeys are rapidly becoming the new standard for secure logins, with more websites and apps jumping on board every day. Curious about which services support passkeys? Check out the FIDO Alliance passkey directory for a list of supported websites and apps.

Why you should start using passkeys in Zoho Vault?

Zoho Vault is more than just a password manager—it’s an all-in-one security solution that combines workforce password management, single sign-on (SSO), and passkey management. With our SAML-based SSO, you can bypass login screens and access cloud apps instantly. Now, with passkeys, your security is even stronger. Just like your passwords, you can sync and organize passkeys across devices, ensuring secure and seamless access from anywhere.

Passkey management made easy for businesses

Zoho Vault makes saving and managing your passkeys easy. Create passkeys for sites like Google, Amazon, and LinkedIn, and store them safely in your Vault. You can organize them with folders and tags, and share them securely with anyone who needs access. Signing in is fast and simple—just choose the passkey option when prompted, and Zoho Vault will handle the rest.

Unlock Zoho Vault with passkeys

Take your passwordless experience to the next level by unlocking Zoho Vault itself with your passkeys, eliminating the need to type in your account password.

Check out our help article for easy-to-follow instructions on how to start using passkeys in Zoho Vault. You'll be up and running in no time!

Secure your digital life—with or without passwords

While passwords have been the standard for years, the era of passwordless authentication is now in full swing. Zoho Vault has stepped up by introducing support for passkeys, offering a secure hub for all of your sensitive information—from traditional passwords and payment cards to documents, secrets, and, now, passkeys.

Whether you're gradually transitioning away from passwords or diving headfirst into the world of passkeys, Zoho Vault is here to guide you every step of the way.

Don’t wait—experience the convenience of passkeys today! Have questions? Reach out to us at support@zohovault.com.
